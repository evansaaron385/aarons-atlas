About Me

I’m an environmental and GIS professional with experience in remote sensing, forestry, environmental data analysis, and regulatory compliance. At Rayonier, I used ArcGIS Pro, LiDAR, elevation data, and aerial imagery to evaluate hydrology and improve environmental mapping across thousands of miles of forestland.

I also bring leadership and emergency-response experience from six years in the Army National Guard as a CBRN NCO. I enjoy using maps, data, and technology to better understand natural resources, communicate environmental conditions, and support responsible decision-making.

Enjoy this atlas full of pictures and adventure through my lifelong journey of learning. Also, all pictured were taken during my travels.
